Blue Flowers

Blues are a color that's a bit challening to come by in the gardening world. The two best flowers for featuring true blues are Blue Poppies are extremely difficult to grow and are pretty hard to come by.Lucky for us, Forget-me-nots are much more forgiving, and they deliver a whole batch of pretty little blue flowers from from spring to summer. They'll do well in full sun or part shade, and should natralize well in your landscape.
